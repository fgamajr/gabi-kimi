1. **Three maturity phases**: ChatTCU (conversational) → GABI Agent (productive) → Audit as Code (paradigm shift)

5. **Bilateral cooperation**: JURISAI framework, INTOSAI partnership, invitation for France to collaborate

**Sources verified**: OECD reports, TCU official publications, Acórdão 616/2024, Portaria-Setid 2/2023, Guia de Uso de IA Generativa, INACIA research papers, LabContas documentation.
