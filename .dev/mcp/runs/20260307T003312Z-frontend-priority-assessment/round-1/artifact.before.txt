Frontend target for GABI DOU:
- Replace the current landing-like home with a true operational dashboard.
- Add an app shell with persistent navigation, mobile-first but strong on desktop.
- Improve search to feel assisted, not just a plain input.
- Upgrade the document reader for very long DOU documents with section-aware reading UX.
- Keep the existing safe image fallback system.
- Prioritize functionality and research workflow over decorative polish.

- Home still has centered hero/title/copy and lacks recent history, richer status, and actionable density.
- Document page does not integrate reading progress, TOC, deep-linking, or saved reading position, despite hooks existing.
- No PDF/share-at-position flow.
- No parser for tabular concurso-style plain text.
- Motion tokens exist but are barely applied.
