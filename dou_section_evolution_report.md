## 7. Key Findings & Digital Transformation Milestones

### Section Launch Timeline
1. **2016-2017: DO1-Only Era**
   - Only DO1 (Executive Acts) was digitally published
   - Legacy transition period for the IN.gov.br portal

2. **February 2018: Major Digital Expansion**
   - DO2 (Private Sector) launched: 313 docs on first day
   - DO3 (Supplemental) launched: 1,176 docs on first day
   - Marks full digital transformation of all DOU sections

3. **2020: COVID-19 Impact**
   - Increased regulatory notices across all sections
   - DO3 peaked at 3,048 docs/day (August 2020)
   - Emergency publications drove volume spikes

4. **2023-2025: Stabilization Phase**
   - Volumes normalized post-pandemic
   - Steady growth patterns established

### Volume Characteristics
- **DO1:** Most stable, government acts published consistently (150-350/day)
- **DO2:** Highest variability, correlates with business cycles (300-975/day)
- **DO3:** Largest volume section, specialized content dominates (1,200-3,000/day)
